What EchoType is

A focused writing utility for people who want text to appear as fast as they can think.

EchoType is not trying to be a full meeting recorder or enterprise call platform. It is a practical voice-to-text tool for personal productivity: quick thoughts, replies, drafts, outlines, and everyday written communication.

Product note

EchoType is intentionally starting narrow so the core workflow can become genuinely useful.

The first job is not to impress with endless features. The first job is to help someone replace small moments of typing with faster spoken text, then make that text usable immediately.

FAQ

Answer the practical questions before people ask them

Does the prototype really work?

Yes. In supported browsers, you can click record, allow microphone access, and see live transcription appear in the editor.

What browser should I use?

The browser version works best in Chromium-based browsers that support the Web Speech API and microphone permissions.

Is this already a finished SaaS?

No. This is a working first product prototype designed to prove the workflow and show what the paid direction could become.

Where is my history stored?

The current prototype stores saved snapshots locally in your browser on this device.
