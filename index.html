Who this is for

Sell to candle makers who already have product signal

Focus on shops with active listings, decent jars or lifestyle photos, and copy that still reads like a placeholder. They are easier to help, easier to close, and easier to upsell into repeat work.

Best fit

  • Candle shops with 10 to 80 active listings
  • Founders who know scent and product but hate writing copy
  • Sellers who want holiday, bridal, or gift-season refreshes

Bad fit

  • Brand new sellers with no reviews, no visuals, and no real market signal
  • Buyers expecting copy alone to fix weak photos or weak products
  • Anyone asking for dozens of listings at bargain pricing
