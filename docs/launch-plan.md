# 7-Day Launch Plan

## Day 1

- Replace placeholder checkout links
- Keep the niche fixed on candles
- Rewrite the hero with your actual product angle and tone

## Day 2

- Prepare 3 sample candle before/after listing rewrites
- Create a simple order intake form
- Set a 48-hour turnaround promise you can actually keep

## Day 3

- DM 30 candle shop owners with weak listings
- Offer one free mini teardown to warm leads
- Link to this page as the service explanation

## Day 4

- Collect objections from replies
- Rewrite pricing and FAQ based on those objections
- Add one testimonial or screenshot if you already have a result

## Day 5

- Deliver the first paid orders fast
- Ask every buyer if they want 2 more scent listings upgraded at a bundle rate

## Day 6

- Package the most common workflow into a template
- Track time spent per order
- Remove any step that does not help the buyer say yes

## Day 7

- Raise the main package if demand is healthy
- Add a premium rush or bundle option
- Decide whether to stay service-first or build more automation
